## Option A – Lisbon on the Way to Madeira (Current Plan)

**Idea:** Get Lisbon time before Madeira; return is a simple connection through Lisbon.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly **Toronto (YYZ) → Porto (OPO)**. Direct.
2. **Jul 24 – Tue Aug 5** – **Mom’s (near Coimbra)**.
3. **Wed Aug 5** – **Train Coimbra → Lisbon**. A **few hours in Lisbon** (Lisete gets Lisbon time).
4. **Wed Aug 5** – **Fly Lisbon → Funchal (LIS→FNC)** afternoon/evening. Multiple daily flights; ~1h35.
5. **Aug 5 – Aug 9/10** – **Madeira**. Accommodation TBD.
6. **Sat Aug 9 or Sun Aug 10** – **Fly Funchal → Toronto** (FNC→LIS→YYZ). One ticket; short connection in Lisbon; **arrive** Toronto Aug 9 or 10 (choose by price).

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train **Coimbra → Lisbon** (Aug 5)
- **Lisbon → Funchal** (Aug 5, afternoon/evening)
- **Funchal → Toronto** (arrive Aug 9 or 10; FNC→LIS→YYZ one ticket)

### Pros

- One clear “Lisbon moment” (few hours) before Madeira.
- Return is one flight day: Madeira → Lisbon → Toronto.
- No extra night in Lisbon on return.

### Cons

- Aug 5 is busy: train + Lisbon + flight same day.
- Only a few hours in Lisbon, not a full day.

## Option B – Porto to Madeira, Then 1 Day in Lisbon on the Return

**Idea:** Fly to Madeira from **Porto**; on the way home, **stay one full day in Lisbon**, then fly to Toronto.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly **Toronto (YYZ) → Porto (OPO)**. Direct.
2. **Jul 24 – e.g. Tue Aug 4 or Wed Aug 5** – **Mom’s (near Coimbra)**.
3. **Tue Aug 4 or Wed Aug 5** – **Train Coimbra → Porto**. Then **fly Porto → Madeira** (OPO→FNC direct if available, or OPO→LIS→FNC). *Check schedules; not all days may have OPO→FNC.*
4. **Madeira** – Stay until day before you want to be in Lisbon.
5. **E.g. Fri Aug 8 or Sat Aug 9** – **Fly Funchal → Lisbon (FNC→LIS)**. **Stay 1 night in Lisbon** (full day + evening in the city).
6. **E.g. Sat Aug 9 or Sun Aug 10** – **Fly Lisbon → Toronto (LIS→YYZ)**. Arrive Toronto Aug 9 or 10.

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train **Coimbra → Porto** (date you leave for Madeira)
- **Porto → Funchal** (OPO→FNC or OPO→LIS→FNC; check availability)
- **Funchal → Lisbon** (FNC→LIS; day you leave Madeira)
- **1 night accommodation in Lisbon**
- **Lisbon → Toronto** (LIS→YYZ; next day; arrive Aug 9 or 10)

### Pros

- **Full day (and night) in Lisbon** on the return.
- Aug 5 is less packed (no train + Lisbon + LIS→FNC same day).
- Porto used for both arrival and departure to Madeira.

### Cons

- Need to confirm **Porto → Madeira** flights (OPO→FNC or OPO→LIS→FNC); may be fewer options than LIS→FNC.
- Extra night in Lisbon (cost + planning).
- Two separate flight bookings for return (FNC→LIS, then LIS→YYZ) unless you use a stopover (e.g. TAP).

## Option C – Porto to Madeira, Return Same Day (No Lisbon Stay)

**Idea:** Fly to Madeira from **Porto**; on the return you only **connect in Lisbon** (no night in the city). No extra Lisbon cost; Aug 5 is not a packed “train + Lisbon + flight” day.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly YYZ → Porto (OPO).
2. **Jul 24 – e.g. Aug 4 or 5** – Mom’s (near Coimbra).
3. **Aug 4 or 5** – Train Coimbra → Porto; fly **Porto → Madeira** (OPO→FNC or OPO→LIS→FNC).
4. **Madeira** – Stay until return.
5. **Aug 9 or 10** – Fly **FNC→LIS→YYZ** (one ticket); arrive Toronto. Lisbon is connection only.

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train Coimbra → Porto; **Porto → Funchal**
- **Funchal → Toronto** (FNC→LIS→YYZ one ticket, arrive Aug 9 or 10)

### Pros

- No busy “train + Lisbon + flight” day; fly to Madeira from Porto.
- No extra night in Lisbon.
- Return is one ticket FNC→LIS→YYZ.

### Cons

- No Lisbon city time (only airport connection).
- Need to confirm Porto→Madeira flight availability.

## Option D – Full Day Lisbon *Before* Madeira, Return Same Day

**Idea:** Get a **full day (and night) in Lisbon** before flying to Madeira; return is same-day connection. Spreads the “Lisbon day” so Aug 5 is not packed.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly YYZ → Porto (OPO).
2. **Jul 24 – Tue Aug 4** – Mom’s (near Coimbra).
3. **Wed Aug 4** – Train Coimbra → Lisbon. **Full day + 1 night in Lisbon.**
4. **Thu Aug 5** – Fly Lisbon → Funchal (LIS→FNC). Rest of day in Madeira.
5. **Aug 5 – Aug 9/10** – Madeira.
6. **Aug 9 or 10** – Fly FNC→LIS→YYZ (one ticket); arrive Toronto.

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train **Coimbra → Lisbon** (Aug 4)
- **1 night accommodation in Lisbon** (Aug 4)
- **Lisbon → Funchal** (Aug 5)
- **Funchal → Toronto** (arrive Aug 9 or 10)

### Pros

- Full day + night in Lisbon before Madeira.
- Aug 5 is only “fly to Madeira,” not train + city + flight.
- Return is one ticket; no extra night on return.

### Cons

- One extra night in Lisbon (cost + booking).
- Leave mom’s one day earlier (Aug 4).

## Option E – Lisbon Before Madeira (Few Hours) + 1 Day Lisbon on Return

**Idea:** **Both** a short Lisbon visit before Madeira **and** a full day in Lisbon on the way home (e.g. TAP stopover or book FNC→LIS one day, LIS→YYZ next). Maximum Lisbon time.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly YYZ → Porto (OPO).
2. **Jul 24 – Tue Aug 5** – Mom’s (near Coimbra).
3. **Wed Aug 5** – Train Coimbra → Lisbon; **few hours in Lisbon**; fly LIS→FNC afternoon/evening.
4. **Aug 5 – e.g. Aug 8 or 9** – Madeira.
5. **e.g. Aug 8 or 9** – Fly FNC → Lisbon; **stay 1 night in Lisbon** (full day + evening).
6. **Aug 9 or 10** – Fly Lisbon → Toronto (LIS→YYZ); arrive Toronto.

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train Coimbra → Lisbon (Aug 5); **LIS → Funchal** (Aug 5 pm)
- **Funchal → Lisbon** (FNC→LIS); **1 night Lisbon**; **Lisbon → Toronto** (arrive Aug 9 or 10)

### Pros

- Lisbon twice: few hours before Madeira + full day on return.
- Good if you want to “see Lisbon” properly on the return.

### Cons

- Aug 5 still busy (train + few hours Lisbon + flight).
- Extra night in Lisbon; two “return” segments (FNC→LIS, then LIS→YYZ).

## Option F – More days in Madeira (leave mom's earlier)

**Idea:** Leave mom’s **Aug 3** (instead of Aug 5), fly to Madeira on **Aug 3**. You get **2 extra days in Madeira**; return is still FNC→LIS→YYZ same day (Aug 9 or 10).

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly YYZ → Porto (OPO).
2. **Jul 24 – Sat Aug 3** – Mom’s (near Coimbra).
3. **Sun Aug 3** – Train Coimbra → Lisbon; few hours in Lisbon; fly LIS→FNC afternoon/evening.
4. **Aug 3 – Aug 9/10** – Madeira (~6–7 days instead of ~4–5).
5. **Aug 9 or 10** – Fly FNC→LIS→YYZ (one ticket); arrive Toronto.

### Bookings

- Toronto → Porto (Jul 23)
- Train Coimbra → Lisbon **(Aug 3)** · **Lisbon → Funchal (Aug 3 pm)** · Funchal → Toronto (arrive Aug 9 or 10)

### Pros

- **More time in Madeira.** Less rush.
- Return is still one ticket; no extra night in Lisbon.

### Cons

- **Two fewer days at mom’s** (leave Aug 3 instead of Aug 5).

## Option G – Two nights in Lisbon on the return

**Idea:** After Madeira, fly FNC→Lisbon and stay **2 nights** in Lisbon (e.g. full day + evening, then a second day). Then fly LIS→YYZ. Best if you want a proper Lisbon mini-break before going home.

### Flow

1. **Wed Jul 23** – Fly YYZ → Porto (OPO).
2. **Jul 24 – Aug 5** (or Aug 4 if flying from Porto) – Mom’s; then to Madeira (as in A or B).
3. **Madeira** – Until you fly to Lisbon (e.g. Aug 7 or 8).
4. **e.g. Aug 7 or 8** – Fly FNC → Lisbon. **Stay 2 nights in Lisbon** (two full days / two evenings).
5. **Aug 9 or 10** – Fly Lisbon → Toronto (LIS→YYZ). Arrive Toronto.

### Bookings

- As per A or B for outbound and Madeira; **Funchal → Lisbon**; **2 nights accommodation in Lisbon**; **Lisbon → Toronto** (arrive Aug 9 or 10).

### Pros

- **Two full days in Lisbon** on the return. Time for day trips or relaxed sightseeing.

### Cons

- Two extra nights in Lisbon (cost + planning). Need to leave Madeira 1–2 days earlier to keep same Toronto arrival date.
